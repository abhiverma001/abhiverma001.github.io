# About Abhishek Verma

## Professional Background

I'm a DevOps engineer dedicated to bridging the gap between development and operations. My passion lies in creating efficient, scalable, and secure infrastructure that empowers teams to deliver software faster and more reliably.

## My Journey

Throughout my career, I've worked on:
- Architecting cloud solutions for startups and enterprises
- Leading infrastructure modernization initiatives
- Mentoring junior engineers in DevOps practices
- Contributing to open-source DevOps projects

## Philosophy

I believe in:
- **Automation First**: Manual processes are the enemy of reliability
- **Infrastructure as Code**: Everything should be version controlled and reproducible
- **Continuous Improvement**: Technology evolves, so should we
- **Collaboration**: DevOps is about breaking silos between teams
- **Security by Design**: Security should be built in, not bolted on
